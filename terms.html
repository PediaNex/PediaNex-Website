Note on business registration: PediaNex is not currently a registered company. Operating as an unregistered sole proprietorship is legal in India for most software businesses, but it means there is no corporate shield between the business and you personally — if a claim is ever brought (e.g. over a data breach or a clinical-data error), your personal assets are not protected the way they would be under an LLP or private limited company. Given this platform stores children's health records, that is a real exposure, not a formality. Before onboarding clinics beyond the founding one, strongly consider: (1) at minimum, Udyam/MSME registration as a sole proprietor, so there's a documented business identity for contracts and grievance-officer purposes, and (2) incorporating an LLP or private limited company, which is the standard structure for liability protection once you're handling other people's patient data commercially. Get this reviewed by a CA or company-secretary alongside the legal review of this document.
