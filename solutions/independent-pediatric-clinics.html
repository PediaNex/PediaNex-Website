Why independent clinics choose PediaNex

Not a scaled-down hospital system. A different starting point.

Most EMR platforms are built for multi-specialty hospitals and adapted downward. PediaNex was built inside a single pediatric clinic first, and only afterward opened to other independent practices — so the defaults already match how a small clinic runs.

No IT team required

PediaNex runs in any browser and installs as an app on phone, tablet, or desktop. There's no server to provision and no in-house IT hire needed to keep it running.

WhatsApp-first, because that's what parents already use

Prescriptions go out as PDFs over WhatsApp instead of print-outs or email, and check-in happens by QR — matching how families already communicate rather than adding a new channel.

India-specific clinical standards, out of the box

Growth charting follows WHO (under-5) and IAP (5–18) standards, and vaccination tracking follows the full IAP immunization schedule — not a generic international default retrofitted for India.

Built for outreach and camps, not just the clinic

An outreach and camp screening mode is built in for practices that also run vaccination camps or school screenings outside the regular clinic setting.

FAQ

Questions independent practices ask first.

Is PediaNex built for solo or small pediatric practices?

Yes. PediaNex started as the internal tool for a single independent pediatric clinic and is designed around how a small practice actually runs a day, not around hospital-scale IT procurement.

Do I need IT staff to set it up?

No. PediaNex runs in any browser and installs as an app on phone, tablet, or desktop, with no server or IT team required on the clinic's side.

Can PediaNex handle outreach camps and school screenings, not just in-clinic visits?

Yes. PediaNex includes an outreach and camp screening mode built for exactly this, alongside the regular in-clinic workflow.

Can it handle Hindi-speaking patients and staff?

Yes. PediaNex supports both English and Hindi.
